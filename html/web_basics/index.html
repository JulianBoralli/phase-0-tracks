A little bit about Jazz

What is Jazz?

Jazz is one of the greatest and most creative music styles! Created in New Orleans at the end of the 19th century, Jazz makes heavy use of improvisation techniques . The style has spread its wings throughout the world and many new styles of music have originate from it.

Some of the most interesting sub-genders of Jazz, in order of importance:

  1. Cool Jazz
  2. Bebop
  3. Modal Jazz
  4. Soul Jazz
  5. Latin Jazz
  6. Free Jazz

Jazz also blended into fusion-genres, such as:

  • Acid Jazz
  • Free Funk
  • Mambo
  • Punk Jazz
  • World Fusion
  • Jam Band
